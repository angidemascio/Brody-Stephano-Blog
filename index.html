AM Transmitter (DSB-SC) Gilbert Cell Modulator

A full AM modulation chain built from discrete BJTs. Two cross-coupled LC oscillators generate the carrier (1.07 MHz) and message (41 kHz) signals, which are then multiplied together by a Gilbert cell mixer to produce a DSB-SC output.

Analog Design · Electronics II

Four-Stage BJT Audio Amplifier

Four stages, all BJTs. A cascode differential pair handles the input, a Wilson current mirror keeps the biasing stable, and a feedback-wrapped power stage drives the 8 Ω load. Got it to 90× linear gain across 30 kHz of bandwidth.
